OUR MISSION

To provide sustainable natural habitats for local pollinator species by making use of unused commercial and urban land around New Jersey.

What we do

Native Plant Restoration: Preserving native plant species to enhance biodiversity and restore natural habitats.

Ecosystem Preservation: Protecting ecosystems for the benefit of current and future generations.

Pollinator Support: Fostering bees, butterflies, moths, and other vital pollinators.

OUR SOLUTION

Finding the space between, behind, and covered up by developments to rewild with pollinator-friendly native plant species.
‍
Creating habitats in urban areas where insect and bird species can find a pure, healthy, and well-kept ecosystem in which to live and grow.
‍
Allowing the public to enjoy natural beauty in their communities and learn about how to protect native habitats.
