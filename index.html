OUR MISSION

To provide sustainable natural ecosystems for local pollinator species by creating habitats on unused commercial and urban land around New Jersey.

What we do

Native Plant Restoration: Creating native pollinator gardens to preserve New Jersey's natural biodiversity.

Ecosystem Preservation: Protecting ecosystems of bees, butterflies, moths, and other vital pollinators.

Urban Rewilding: Transforming urban land into vibrant native gardens.

Growing Environmental Hazards

Overdevelopment

Pollution

Pesticides

OUR SOLUTION

Finding the space between, behind, and covered up by developments to rewild with pollinator-friendly native plant species.
‍
Creating habitats in urban areas where insect and bird species can find a pure, healthy, and well-kept ecosystem in which to live and grow.
‍
Allowing the public to enjoy natural beauty in their communities and learn about how to protect native habitats.
