You Can Sponsor a Pollinator Garden

Beautify your land by sponsoring a pollinator garden from the NBI! Our members and volunteers will come to your location and create a native plant garden at no cost. Native plants require very little maintenance, and will help pollinators for long into the future.

Whether at your home or at your business, any unused piece of land can help support vital species. Once you fill out this form, we will be in touch with you shortly to begin a conversation about creating a garden.

You can also reach out at nativebloominitiative@gmail.com
